This yet I apprehend not, why to those Among whom God will deigne to dwell on Earth So many and so various Laws are giv’n; So many Laws argue so many sins Among them; how can God with such reside?

To whom thus Michael. Doubt not but that sin Will reign among them, as of thee begot; And therefore was Law given them to evince Thir natural pravitie, by stirring up Sin against Law to fight;
