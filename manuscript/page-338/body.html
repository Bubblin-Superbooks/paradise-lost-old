Silence, ye troubl’d waves, and thou Deep, peace, Said then th’ Omnific Word, your discord end:

Nor staid, but on the Wings of Cherubim Uplifted, in Paternal Glorie rode Farr into Chaos, and the World unborn; For Chaos heard his voice: him all his Traine Follow’d in bright procession to behold Creation, and the wonders of his might.
