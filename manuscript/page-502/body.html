Then Heav’n and Earth renewd shall be made pure To sanctitie that shall receive no staine: Till then the Curse pronounc’t on both precedes.

Hee ended, and the heav’nly Audience loud Sung Halleluia, as the sound of Seas, Through multitude that sung: Just are thy ways, Righteous are thy Decrees on all thy Works; Who can extenuate thee? Next, to the Son, Destin’d restorer of Mankind, by whom New Heav’
