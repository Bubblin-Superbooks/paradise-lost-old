But if the sense of touch whereby mankind Is propagated seem such dear delight Beyond all other, think the same voutsaf’t To Cattel and each Beast; which would not be To them made common & divulg’d, if aught Therein enjoy’d were worthy to subdue The Soule of Man, or passion in him move. What higher in her societie thou findst Attractive, human, rational, love still; In loving thou dost well, in passion not, Wherein true Love consists not;
