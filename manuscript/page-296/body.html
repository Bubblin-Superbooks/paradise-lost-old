Far otherwise th’ inviolable Saints In Cubic Phalanx firm advanc’t entire, Invulnerable, impenitrably arm’d: Such high advantages thir innocence Gave them above thir foes, not to have sinnd, Not to have disobei’d; in fight they stood Unwearied, unobnoxious to be pain’d By wound, though from thir place by violence mov’d.
