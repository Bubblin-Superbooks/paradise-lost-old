He said, and on his Son with Rayes direct Shon full, he all his Father full exprest Ineffably into his face receiv’d, And thus the filial Godhead answering spake.

O Father, O Supream of heav’nly Thrones, First, Highest, Holiest, Best, thou alwayes seekst To glorifie thy Son, I alwayes thee, As is most just; this I my Glorie account, My exaltation, and my whole delight, That thou in me well pleas’d, declarst thy will Fulfill’d, which to fulfil is all my bliss.
