So spake our Sire, and by his count’nance seemd Entring on studious thoughts abstruse, which Eve Perceaving where she sat retir’d in sight, With lowliness Majestic from her seat, And Grace that won who saw to wish her stay, Rose, and went forth among her Fruits and Flours, To visit how they prosper’
