At length a Reverend Sire among them came, And of thir doings great dislike declar’d, And testifi’d against thir wayes; hee oft Frequented thir Assemblies, whereso met, Triumphs or Festivals, and to them preachd Conversion and Repentance, as to Souls In prison under Judgements imminent: But all in vain: which when he saw, he ceas’d Contending, and remov’d his Tents farr off; Then from the Mountain hewing Timber tall, Began to build a Vessel of huge bulk, Measur’d by Cubit, length, & breadth, and highth, Smeard round with Pitch, and in the side a dore Contriv’
